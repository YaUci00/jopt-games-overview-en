About JOPT Games
Executive Summary

JOPT Games is a mobile-exclusive app designed for players to win seats to JOPT — Japan's premier live poker events — entirely online. Rather than a standalone in-app gaming experience, the product is purpose-built as a seamless pipeline: Online Qualifier → Live Tournament Entry.

Built on a globally proven technology stack and tournament operation engine, the platform operates on a strict non-real-money basis — chips cannot be exchanged for cash. Designed for Japan, Korea, and Asia, players across the region compete on the same pipeline for seats to JOPT, SPADIE, Sengoku Poker Tour, APT (Asian Poker Tour), and more. Through the WSOP (World Series of Poker) partnership, Japanese players gain a direct route to the World Series in Las Vegas.

Product Foundation
Platform Foundation

JOPT Games is built on a globally proven technology stack and tournament management engine, delivering online pathways to live poker seat acquisition. The platform operates on a strict non-real-money basis — in-app chips are non-cashable. Rather than restricting the player pool to Japan alone, the platform is designed for expansion across Asia, allowing players from Japan, Korea, and beyond to compete for event seats and Day 1 entries on the same pipeline.

Asia-Ready · Asia-wide Player Pool
Not a closed Japanese-only environment
Tournaments match players across Japan, Korea, and Asia in the same event, competing for the same entry rights. All operations are non-real-money — app chips cannot be cashed out and no cash prizes are distributed directly.
SSO · Direct Integration with WSOP Live
One login, two platforms
Shared account system means WSOP satellite seats won through JOPT Games are received and used directly within the WSOP Live app — no new account required. The entire player experience is completed with a single login.
Player Value Proposition: Enjoy globally-standard tournament quality while managing seat rights for events across Japan, Korea, and Asia — all from a single pipeline. The non-real-money model lowers the barrier to competitive participation, while top players are provided an exit route to WSOP, APT, KSOP, and other world and Asian championships.
03
Two Core Functions
Function Architecture

JOPT Games is organized around two distinct functional pillars, each serving a different purpose.

① Satellite Function — Online → Live Pipeline Engine
Win your way into live tournaments
The core feature through which players progress through online qualifiers to win seats at live championship events. A four-stage structure — Trial → Satellite → Day 1 → Day 2 — lets players choose their entry point based on budget and skill level.

Unified qualifier pathways for JOPT, SPADIE, Sengoku Poker Tour, APT, and more — all managed from a single app.
② Club Function — Player Community Operations
Your home for competitive community play
Players create clubs and run tournaments, ring games, and leaderboards within their community. This digitally recreates Japan's poker culture of competitive play among friends and peers.

Club-level records and ranking data are exportable, with planned integration for venue operations and local tournament tie-ins.

06
Direct Buy-In
Direct Buy-In

Separate from subscriptions, players can purchase event participation tickets directly through an e-commerce storefront and enter targeted tournaments without a subscription.

EC Ticket — Direct Ticket Sales
No subscription required
Players purchase the tickets they need directly from the e-commerce site. Separate purchase pathways are available for each event — JOPT, SPADIE, Sengoku Poker Tour, APT, and more — catering to players with a specific tournament in mind.
Role Design — Subscription vs. Direct
Two entry points, one pipeline
Subscriptions serve players who want to play consistently. Direct buy-ins serve players targeting a specific event. Offering both channels ensures the platform captures everyone — from casual players to championship-focused competitors.
Design Note: EC ticket sales are structured as a purchase pathway for event seats and participation rights — separate from the in-app non-real-money framework. After purchase, tickets sync with the player's in-app entry information, so they can proceed directly to their target event without confusion.
