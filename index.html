01About JOPT Games
EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

JOPT Games is a mobile-exclusive app designed for players to win seats to JOPT — Japan's premier live poker events — entirely online. Rather than a standalone in-app gaming experience, the product is purpose-built as a seamless pipeline: Online Qualifier → Live Tournament Entry.

Built on a globally proven technology stack and tournament operation engine, the platform operates on a strict non-real-money basis — chips cannot be exchanged for cash. Designed for Japan, Korea, and Asia, players across the region compete on the same pipeline for seats to JOPT, SPADIE, Sengoku Poker Tour, APT (Asian Poker Tour), and more. Through the WSOP (World Series of Poker) partnership, Japanese players gain a direct route to the World Series in Las Vegas.

02Product Foundation
PLATFORM FOUNDATION

JOPT Games is an online pipeline to live poker event seats, built on a globally proven technology stack and tournament operations engine. The platform runs on a non-real-money basis — chips are non-cashable. The player pool is not restricted to Japan; it is designed for expansion across Asia, with players from Japan, Korea, and beyond competing for the same event entries on one unified pipeline.

ASIA-READY
Asia-Ready Player Pool

JOPT Games tournaments are not a closed, Japan-only environment. The platform is built to match players from Japan, Korea, and across Asia in the same events, competing for the same entry rights. All operations run on a non-real-money basis (no wagering) — in-app chips are non-cashable and no cash prizes are distributed directly.

SSO
Direct Connection to WSOP Live

Shared account system means WSOP satellite seats won through JOPT Games are received and used directly within the WSOP Live app — no new account required. The entire player experience is completed with a single login.

Player Value Proposition

Enjoy globally standard tournament quality while managing seat rights for events across Japan, Korea, and Asia — all from a single pipeline. The non-real-money model lowers the barrier to competitive participation, while top players have a clear exit route to WSOP, APT, KSOP, and other world and Asian championships.

03Two Core Functions
FUNCTION ARCHITECTURE

JOPT Games is organized around two distinct functional pillars, each serving a different purpose.

①
Satellite Function
ONLINE → LIVE Pipeline Engine

The core feature through which players advance through online qualifiers to win seats at live championship events. A four-stage structure — Trial → Satellite → Day 1 → Day 2 — lets players choose their entry point based on budget and skill level.

Unified qualifier pathways for JOPT, SPADIE, Sengoku Poker Tour, APT, and more — all managed from a single app.

②
Club Function
Player Community Operations

Players create clubs and run tournaments, ring games, and leaderboards within their community. This digitally recreates Japan's poker culture of competitive play among friends and peers.

Club-level records and ranking data are exportable, with planned integration for venue operations and local tournament tie-ins.

Flexible Entry Design

Players are not limited to advancing sequentially — direct entry routes are available at every layer. Based on budget, schedule, and skill level, each player can choose the optimal starting point for their competitive journey.

Responsible Gaming Policy

JOPT Games operates on a non-real-money basis (no wagering). In-app chips are non-cashable, and prizes awarded through satellites and qualifiers are limited to event seats and participation tickets — no cash bets are placed and no cash prizes are distributed through the app.

06Direct Buy-In
DIRECT BUY-IN

Players can also purchase event participation tickets directly through an e-commerce storefront — no subscription required.

EC TICKET
Direct Ticket Sales

Players can purchase the tickets they need directly from the e-commerce site without a subscription. Separate purchase pathways are available for each event — JOPT, SPADIE, Sengoku Poker Tour, APT, and more — catering to players with a specific tournament in mind.

ROLE DESIGN
Subscription vs. Direct Buy-In

Subscriptions serve players who want to compete consistently. Direct buy-ins serve players targeting a specific event. Offering both channels ensures the platform captures everyone — from casual players to championship-focused competitors.

Design Note

EC ticket sales are structured as a purchase pathway for event seats and participation rights — separate from the in-app non-real-money framework. After purchase, tickets sync with the player's in-app entry information, so they can proceed directly to their target event without confusion.
